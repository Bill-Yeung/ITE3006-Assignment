Travel Tips for Hong Kong

Information to make your trip smooth and enjoyable

Language

Commonly used languages are Chinese (Cantonese), Chinese (Putonghua) and English.

Transportation

The MTR is a convenient way to get around the city. Buses, trams, and ferries are also affordable options.

Download the MTR mobile app

Weather

Hong Kong has a subtropical climate, so be prepared for hot and humid summers and mild winters.

Check the latest weather

Culture

Respect local customs and traditions, especially when visiting temples or religious sites.

Payment Methods

Credit cards are widely accepted, but carry some cash for small purchases. The Octopus card is essential for public transport and many shops.
